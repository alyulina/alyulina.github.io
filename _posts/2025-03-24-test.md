## Hamiltonian  
the Ising model has a Hamiltonian in terms of spin variables given by[^1]  
$$ H  = -J \sum_{\langle ij\rangle} \sigma_i\sigma_j + \sum_i h_i \sigma_i$$

[^1]: See e.g. [this link](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ising_model).
